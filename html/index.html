CYOA

CYOA - Choose your own adventure, is typically text based adventure game, where the player gets to make choices for the character they are controlling, usually in a fantasy setting. These kind of games first gathered popularity in the form of a book. Once migrated onto digital plane they still consist mostly of plain text with minimal design elements.

Here is my version of such adventure where player takes the reigns of not-so-fictiona character who's name is Paan. And yes, he is me. Objective of the player is to guide this not-so-fictional character to find his path to a certain location - the school. And the clock is ticking..
